Cranberry Sauce Muffins

Description

These leftover cranberry sauce muffins are the perfect way to make the most of every last bit of your Thanksgiving meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup leftover cranberry sauce
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line 18 muffin cups with paper liners.
  2. Beat cranberry sauce, milk, oil, egg, and vanilla together in a bowl until well combined.
  3. Whisk flour, brown sugar, white sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, cardamom, and salt together in a separate bowl.
  4. Stir dry ingredients into wet ingredients until batter is just moistened. Pour into the prepared muffin cups.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
